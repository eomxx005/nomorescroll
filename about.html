For parents

A place where children aged 5-10 can play a calm board game, then go back to their day.

Our current promise

  • No ads right now. If we ever add them later, only quiet, non-tracking ones — and we'll say so up front.
  • No sign-up. We don't ask for email, phone, or any personal info.
  • No analytics, no tracking. Not even Google Analytics.
  • No recommendation algorithm. The 8 games on the home page are all there is.
  • No autoplay videos.
  • No infinite scroll.
  • No design tricks to keep your child on screen longer.

The 8 games

Tic-Tac-Toe, Nim, Connect Four, Gomoku, Chess, Reversi, Go, and a Korean-only Baduk learning game. All centuries-old board games. A round takes 1 to 20 minutes — then it's done.

Why this exists

We didn't want our kids stuck in the infinite-scroll loop. But cutting screens out completely isn't realistic either. So we collected the kind of games that ask you to think one move at a time, in one quiet place.

Cost

Free. Right now there are no ads and no paid features. If running costs grow, we may add light, non-tracking ads later, and we'll be honest about it on this page.

Offline

Each game keeps working once it's loaded, even if the internet drops. Useful in a car, on a plane, or just on patchy Wi-Fi.
