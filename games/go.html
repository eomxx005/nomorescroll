📖 Quick Rules
Go in 60 seconds!
1
Place stones on intersections

Click any dot where lines cross. Stones stay put once placed.

2
Liberties keep stones alive

Surround all neighbor spaces of an enemy group to capture it!

3
No suicide moves

You can't place a stone with zero liberties unless it captures an enemy group.

4
Ko rule

You can't immediately recapture the exact stone just taken.

5
Score = Territory + Captures

Most points wins! 🏆
