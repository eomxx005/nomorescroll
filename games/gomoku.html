📖 Quick Rules
Gomoku in 60 seconds!
1
Place stones on intersections

Click any point where lines cross. Stones stay put once placed.

2
Get five in a row

Connect five of your stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to win!

3
Black goes first

You play Black and go first every game. The computer plays White.

4
Block your opponent

Stop the computer from getting five in a row, or you lose!
