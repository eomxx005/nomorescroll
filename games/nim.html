📖 Nim Rules
How to play!
1
Take from one row

On your turn, take 1 or more matches from exactly one row.

2
Last match wins

The player who takes the very last match wins the game!

3
No skipping

You must take at least one match on your turn.

4
XOR strategy

XOR all pile sizes. A non-zero XOR means there is a winning move!
